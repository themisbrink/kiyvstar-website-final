How to participate

  • 1Send YES to 900 and join the SMS campaign (cost of 1 SMS – UAH 7.50 including VAT and PF). Shortly after, you will receive a welcome message introducing to you the game and your first 20 points

  • 2Right after, you will receive a message with one question and two possible answers, the one is correct and the other is wrong. You will have to send the number of the correct answer (either 1 or 2) to number 900

  • 3 In the following message, you will be informed that the last question you answered is either correct or not, the points earned and the next question is coming

FAQ

What is the duration of the campaign?

The Campaign "SMS for million" starts on the 23th of November 2015 at 00:00:01 and ends on 20th of March 2016 at 23:59:59. The total duration of Campaign is 119 days.

How can I participate?

It is very easy to join the campaign "SMS for million”.


1Send an SMS-message with the word "YES" to the number 900 (the cost of one SMS - 7,50 UAH inclusive all taxes.


2You will then receive a welcome message and the first 20 points.


3Next, you have to answer campaign questions, by sending SMS. Questions and answer choices will be given in the SMS. Choose your answer and send a number (1 or 2), which corresponds to the chosen answer to the number 900. The cost of one SMS - 7,50UAH inclusive all taxes.


4With each answer you will receive points. Even if you answer incorrectly, the given points will still increase your score.


5Continue to answer questions and gather points to get closer to victory! You can always win the grand prize - 1 000 000 UAH!

What are the prizes?

Weekly Prizes:
Two prizes – 100 000 UAH each – will be given to two participants of the campaign, accordingly to the Terms and Conditions.



Grand Prizes:
1 000 000 UAH will be awarded to the winner of the campaign, according to its Terms and Conditions.

How will I know that I won an extra prize?

You will be informed via SMS during the campaign.

Can a winner of a prize win again another prize?

Each participant can receive one weekly prize, and become the owner of the Grand Prize. Thus, you can get 2 prizes - one from each category.

What is the Subscription service ?

By activating the “Subscription” service, you will receive additional points on daily basis, that will be added to your score and participate in the campaign.

How to activate the “Subscription” service?

Send a free SMS-message with the word "OK" to number 901 to order and activate the "Subscription" service. If activation is successful, you will get 1 free week, during which you will be awarded with additional points. Thanks to them, you will be able to accumulate more points and get closer to winning the Grand Prize and other prizes!

Service “Subscription” is available for free?

The free period lasts 7 days after service activation. During this period, you will get free points to increase the score to become a winner.


7 days after the activation of "Subscription" service cost will become UAH 2.50 (inclusive all taxes) till 20.03.2016 inclusive, unless you unsubscribe from the service earlier. The service can also be extended and all the participants of the “Subscription” service will be informed via an SMS and on the campaign’s website.

How to opt-out from “Subscription” service?

You can disable the "Subscription" service by sending a free SMS with the word "STOP" to number 901.

I don't want to receive SMS with questions any more.

Send a free SMS with the word "STOP" to number 991 and you will no longer receive sms-messages with questions. Your points amount will no change, and you can still win prizes. You can restore participation in the campaign by sending SMS-messages to 900.

I am not a subscriber of Kyivstar, may I participate in the Campaign?

Participants of the campaign can only be Kyivstar subscribers. If you do not have a Kyivstar number yet, purchase a prepaid starter package and join the campaign. The first thing you have to do - send an SMS-message with the word «OK» to the number 900. Tthe cost of one SMS - 7,50 UAH inclusive all taxes.

How can I learn how many points I have collected so far?

To check the amount of points, just send the word “score” to 991, free of charge.

May I change the language of the messages?

For Ukrainian language, please send the word “UKR” to 991, free of charge. For Russian - the word “RUS”.
